Services

Pressure Logic provides a wide range of professional pressure washing services and can meet almost any need of residential homeowners here in Northeast Ohio

Check out our list of services below:

  • House Wash - Our safe, low pressure, soft wash cleaning process utilizes our house wash solution that washes away dirt, algae, mildew and lichen and will leave your house looking its very best.
  • Roof Cleaning - Wash away years of black streaks, moss, algae and lichen and have your roof looking like new with a treatment of our roof cleaning solution. We apply our roof cleaning solution using low-pressure in order to not damage the roofing material. This is an essential service in order to stop organic growth and preserve your roof for the future.
  • Concrete Cleaning - Concrete driveways, walkways, patios and pool decks are cleaned utilizing a three-step cleaning process which washes away dirt and grime while eliminating algae and mildew at the source, leaving the concrete surface bright and fresh.
